What design direction should we take?

Your current site uses a dark luxury aesthetic with gold accents. Should we evolve it or go in a new direction?

A

Refined Dark Luxury (Evolution)

Keep the dark background + gold accent foundation but elevate it significantly. Better typography hierarchy, more whitespace, cinematic imagery, smoother animations. Think: Rolex meets industrial manufacturing. The current direction taken to a premium level.

Dark + Gold Cinematic Bold CTAs
B

Clean Industrial Modern

Switch to a light/white primary background with dark accents. Clean, airy, professional — like a high-end B2B SaaS or architecture firm. Generous whitespace, grid-based layouts, photography-forward. Think: Apple meets premium manufacturing.

Light + Clean Airy Dark Accents
C

Bold & Dynamic

High-contrast, energetic design with large typography, asymmetric layouts, scroll-driven animations, and strong visual storytelling. Think: Stripe meets industrial. More tech-forward and attention-grabbing than either option above.

Dark Gradients Bold Color Dynamic

Note: Your audience is sign shop owners and trade professionals — they value credibility, product quality, and speed. The design should feel premium and trustworthy, not flashy or consumer-oriented.
